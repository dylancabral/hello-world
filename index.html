Spiritbox

Spirit Box is a Progressive metalcore band from victoria island british columbie. Husband and wife duo use pop structure to turn criminally heavy guitars and drums into palatable and tasteful combination of Heavy, Beautiful and downright catchy music.

Invent Animate

Invent Animate incorporates elements of metalcore, progressive metalcore, progressive metal, ambient, djent, and post-hardcore in their music. They often feature start-and-stop arrangements of calm atmospheric passages and intense metalcore breakdowns

DARKO US

Darko US, a “supergroup” from deathcore creative minds Josh Miller (Emmure) and Tom Barber (Chelsea Grin), showcases their aggressive side by attempting to craft an album of sheer brutality and heaviness that remains relentless throughout its duration
